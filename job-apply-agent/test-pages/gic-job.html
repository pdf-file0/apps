GIC Internship Programme

The GIC Internship Programme is an internship programme covering multiple tracks for undergraduates. Investment Professionals internships span public and private markets: public equities, fixed income, private equity, infrastructure and real estate. Positions are also available in Technology and Corporate Services.

Applicants indicate a preferred track during the application, and responsibilities differ by track — from investment research in public markets to due diligence on direct investments in private markets.
