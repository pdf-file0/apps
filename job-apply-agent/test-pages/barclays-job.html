Research Analyst Summer Internship Programme 2027 Singapore

Barclays Research provides fundamental analysis across asset classes for institutional investor clients. As a Summer Analyst in the Research team you will help develop investment theses on interest rates, FX, commodities and currencies, contribute to thematic research and macro analysis, and build financial models for companies under coverage.

You will support equity research coverage and cross-asset research writing, publish notes under the guidance of senior analysts, and interact daily with the sales and trading desks that serve markets clients across the region.
